PROJECT 03

Recruiting Metrics Dashboard @ Headway

As Talent Operations Lead during a period of rapid company growth, I built a weekly reporting cadence that tracked time to hire, interviewer scorecard completion, and top of funnel pass through rates. This gave TA leadership a consistent, data backed view of where the pipeline was working, and where it was leaking, and directly informed sourcing strategy during the company's highest growth quarters.
