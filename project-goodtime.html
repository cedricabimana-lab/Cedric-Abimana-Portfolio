Preparing For Your Software Engineering Interview!

Congratulations on making it to the final interview rounds at The Trade Desk!

This portal is designed to help you feel confident and prepared for your upcoming interviews. Be sure to review the attached Software Development Engineer Interview Prep Guide, which outlines what to expect, four 60 minute interviews with our engineering team, including coding, system design, and a behavioral style interview to assess your leadership and management style. You'll also find tips on how to approach problem solving, what our interviewers look for, and links to helpful prep resources.

What to Expect:

  • Prep Call with Your Recruiter - Before your interview day, you'll have a 1:1 prep call with your recruiter. This is your chance to walk through your schedule, get clarity on the interview format, and ask any lingering questions, we're here to set you up for success.
  • Dress Code - No need to suit up! Our dress code is casual. Wear whatever makes you feel comfortable and confident.
  • Bringing a Laptop - Some of our interviews include live coding, and it's easiest (and most comfortable) to work from your own machine. Don't Have a Laptop? No worries! Just let us know at least 48 hours in advance and we'll arrange a loaner laptop for you during your onsite visit.
  • Requesting Accommodations - We're committed to making our interview process inclusive and accessible to all. If you need accommodations, please reach out to accommodations@thetradedesk.com or connect with your recruiter directly.

Who to Contact?

Your Recruiter is your go to for:

  • Interview format and structure.
  • What to expect in each round.
  • Feedback or outcomes after your interviews.
  • Questions about the interview panel or technical focus areas.

Your Candidate Experience Partner (CEP) is your main point of contact for:

  • Scheduling, calendar invites, ZOOM issues, and last minute conflicts / reschedules.
  • Onsite visit logistics, travel, and reimbursements (if applicable).
  • Laptop and tech setup.

Here you will find our Company Fact Sheet, a handy snapshot of who we are, what we do, and why it matters.
